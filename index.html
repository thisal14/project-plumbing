Emergency Preparedness

What To Do While You Wait

Before our team arrives, follow these steps to minimize damage and ensure safety.

1

Stop the water flow — Locate and turn off the main water isolation valve immediately.

2

Contain the leak — Use buckets, towels, or a wet vacuum to manage pooling water.

3

Shut off power — If water is near electrical outlets, turn off the mains power supply.

4

Clear the area — Move furniture, rugs, and valuables away from the affected zone.

5

Document everything — Take photos for insurance purposes before we begin work.

6

Call us immediately — Dial 0434 055 924 to confirm your location.
